# **App Name**: Matsu

## Core Features:

- AI Content Hooks: An integrated AI tool that generates engaging video titles and captions based on provided video descriptions.
- Interactive Video Portfolio: A liquid glass video gallery with category filtering (Basic, Complex, Sound Design) and smooth hover animations.
- Service Showcases: Three detailed service cards (Basic, Complex, Sound Design) featuring the Liquid Glass aesthetic with frost and glow effects.
- Liquid Hero Section: High-impact hero section with blurred background-filter effects and a clear call-to-action leading to the portfolio.
- Glass Contact Portal: Minimalist contact form styled with backdrop-filter: blur and border-glow, allowing clients to send project inquiries.
- Social Connect Footer: Direct social platform integration with Instagram, TikTok, and YouTube iconography for professional credibility.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Silver Mercury (#E5E5E5), providing high-contrast text and bright highlights against a dark scheme.
- Background color: Deep Onyx (#0D0D0D), serving as the foundational layer for the liquid glass transparency effects.
- Accent color: Stealth Gray (#999999), used for subtle shadows, borders, and UI element variations.
- The font 'Inter' (sans-serif) will be used for both headlines and body text to maintain a modern, objective, and neutral aesthetic.
- A sleek one-page continuous scroll layout utilizing mobile-first responsiveness and a fixed navigation bar with glassmorphism.
- Subtle fade-in scroll animations using entry viewports and card-level liquid glass hover transitions (saturate + blur).
- Minimalist vector icons for platforms and services with semi-transparent frosted glass backing elements.
